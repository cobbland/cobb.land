I've been trying not to buy new games (the size of my Steam library makes me blush), so despite Titanfall 2 having been on sale for $2 for the better part of the last decade, I haven't played it yet. Then it was gifted to me, so I no longer had an excuse. (Thanks, Jordan!)

This game is a game game, if you know what I mean. It's part Halo, part Half-Life, and part Call of Duty. (We'll forgive it that last part.) The running and gunning sections were nicely spaced by wall running and platforming sections. Eventually, there's almost even a puzzle or two. Almost.

I'd say it went a little too far with the set pieces, especially in the last act. They were good—don't get me wrong. But sometimes you just want to play a game, ya know? And I find myself being less and less into shootie games as I age. Despite those quibbles, I enjoyed Titanfall 2 all the way through. **I recommend it.**
