*Star Wars: Outlaws* was a good game that could have been great. It was close. The bones were great. And so was the muscle. But there was just too much fat on that meat.

While the open world and side quests started out fun (the aforementioned fat), both grew tedious eventually. Follow the quest marker, sneak in, do the thing, fight your way out. Again and again. Granted, there were a few side quests without map markers where you had to puzzle out the location. I would have loved those if it weren't for the fact that they slowed me down completing my mile-long check list of missions. But maybe that's a me problem. 

Were I to have just followed the main quests and finished the game in under twenty hours, then messed around with side quests after until I got bored, I think I would have loved this game. It could have been better. As it is though, I still like it a lot. **I recommend it.**
