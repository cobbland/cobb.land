# Words Mean Things

When playing D&D, don't call your own creations "homebrew". The stuff you make is as much D&D as anything.

When installing programs on your smartphone from outside the "offical" app store, don't call it "sideloading". You're just installing programs like we've been doing for decades.

Words mean things. When we use words that make what we want to do sound like something strange or niche or outside the norm, we discourage ourselves and others from doing them---these perfectly normal things.
