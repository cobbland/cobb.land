> 十亿人民九亿麻，还有一亿在观察
> 
> Among a billion people, nine hundred million are playing mahjong, and the rest are watching.

<p class="note">— Chinese saying taken from <b>Mahjong for Dummies</b></p>

From smoke-filled parlors to kitchen tables, [Mahjong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mahjong) is a game with a long history that spans countries and centuries. There are many variations of mahjong. Here, I've collected some resources for learning and playing [old Hong Kong mahjong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mahjong#Old_Hong_Kong_mahjong_rules)<sup><a href=#1down id="1up">1</a></sup> (and [riichi mahjong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese_mahjong), marked with a fleuron).

## Learn

- [Old Hong Kong mahjong rules](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mahjong#Old_Hong_Kong_mahjong_rules) and [scoring](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong_mahjong_scoring_rules) from Wikipedia
- ❁[Riichi mahjong rules](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese_mahjong) and [scoring](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese_mahjong_scoring_rules) also from Wikipedia
- [Green Tile Social Club](https://greentilesocialclub.com)'s ["Setup + Play Instructions" and "Official Points System"](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1KgEhocgEq7Nn2seAn1rXC2m1ITOJKfvt) on Google Drive
- [Victoria Mahjong Club's "Resources" page](https://victoriamahjongclub.com/resources) contains cheat sheets and a beginner's guide
- [Mahjong 101: A Beginner’s Crash Course (Hong Kong Style)](https://youtu.be/qHk2j7FdcuI) from [Wong Side of History](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCsdZ-heFNbV3pKXLyLcg3IA) on YouTube
- [How to Mahjong (Basic Hong Kong style)](https://youtu.be/_JGsb87PEIo) and [How to Mahjong with points (Advanced Hong Kong style)](https://youtu.be/qVQyV7OMWdA) from [Mahjong Sparrow](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCnUDlWrYto6WP51wdYrmgcg) also on YouTube
