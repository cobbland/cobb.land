Here is a collection of resources that I—an absolute beginner on the topic—am building as I research socialism and communism. Take it for what it is.

## Books

From [The only leftist reading list you'll ever need (lol)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QdwD7zwEpZw):

- Marx and Engles
    - [The Principles of Communism](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1847/11/prin-com.htm) by Friedrich Engels
    - [Wage Labour and Capital](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1847/wage-labour/) by Karl Marx
    - [Value, Price and Profit](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1865/value-price-profit/) by Marx
    - [Socialism: Utopian and Scientific](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1880/soc-utop/index.htm) by Engels
    - (honorable mention: [Manifesto of the Communist Party](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1848/communist-manifesto/) just to say you did it)
- Lenin
    - [The State and Revolution](https://www.marxists.org/archive/lenin/works/1917/staterev/) by Vladimir Lenin 
    - [Imperialism, the Highest Stage of Capitalism](https://www.marxists.org/archive/lenin/works/1916/imp-hsc/) by Lenin 
    - (honorable mention: *Basics of Leninism; Dialectical and Historical Materialism*)
- Mao 
    - [On Practice](https://www.marxists.org/reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-1/mswv1_16.htm) by Mao Tse-tung 
    - [On Contradiction](https://www.marxists.org/reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-1/mswv1_17.htm) by Mao 
    - [Oppose Book Worship](https://www.marxists.org/reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-6/mswv6_11.htm) by Mao 
    - [Combat Liberalism](https://www.marxists.org/reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-2/mswv2_03.htm) by Mao
